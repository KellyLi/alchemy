What is ExoShield?

ExoShield is our premium product for windshield protection. A thin transparent film created to withstand collision from debris and stones up to 140 KM/h which is 6 times the protection of just your windshield alone. Created with durable and UV protecting nanocomposites, the ExoShield also protects against 99.9% of UV radiation.

Do Wipers Scratch the Film?

Proper care of your wiper blades will keep your film in optimal condition and will avoid sand or debris from getting caught in between the wiper and the film. Please refer to our maintenance guide for details as to how to properly take care of the film and your wiper blades.

Does the ExoShield Obstruct Visibility?

The ExoShield has an 88% VLT which means it is within road safe regulations in the US and Canada and will not obstruct your visibility on the road, during the day or night.

How Durable is the ExoShield?

Every ExoShield is tested in our facility for scratch resistance, abrasion resistance and impact resistance. We have found that with proper maintenance of your wipers and windshield your ExoShield has a guarantee to last up to two years or 50,000 KM/ 31,068 miles.

Clean your wipers with a solution of water or 1 part vinegar and 3 parts water. Just soak a paper and run against the rubber part of the wipers.
