Introducing ExoShield

A thin, transparent film powered by nanotechnology to bring your windshield into the 21st Century

ExoShield is applied on the exterior surface of a windshield to augment it with five innovative, cost-saving properties

Stone Chip Prevention

ExoShield absorbs the impact energy from stones hitting your windshield to prevent stone chips and windshield breakage.

UV Rejection

ExoShield block 99.9% of harmful UV radiation to help reduce risk of skin cancer for all drivers and passengers.

Improved Visibility

ExoShield reduce snow, ice, and water adhesion by 60% to improve your visibility and keep you safer during harsh weather conditions.

Heat Reduction

ExoShield Solar Only

ExoShield rejects 90% of solar infrared radiation to keep vehicles cooler, drivers comfortable, and reduce A/C fuel consumption by 20%.

Frost Prevention

ExoShield AntiFrost Only

ExoShield keeps windshields frost-free so you no longer have to scrape every winter morning and drive with a clear view of the road.
